BORDENTOWN BASEBALL - HOME

Welcome to the Bordentown Baseball website! On this site, you will be able to find any and all information regarding the Bordentown Baseball team for the 2018 season. From team information, to the upcoming schedule, to the results of previous games, this is the go-to site for finding out anything you need to know about this team.

The Team Information Page will allow you to be able to find the roster of players, as well as coaches. Along with the names of the players and coaches, you will be able to find their numbers, as well as their graduation years. The Team Information page will also provide you with player statistics form the 2018 baseball season.

The Standings Page will allow you to find out how the Bordentown Baseball team ranks compared to their competitors. Bordentown is in the Patriot Division for baseball, which includes such rivals as Delran and Holy Cross. Bordentown is in Central Jersey Group 2 when it comes to rankings for the Sectional Tournament, which occurs at the end of every season. This pits them against such teams as Governor Livingston, Voorhees, and Metuchen.

The Schedule Page will help you find information regarding upcoming games to be played by the Bordentown Baseball team. The team typically plays about 25 games in a season. Using this page, you will be able to see the date of the game, the start time of the game, the opponent to be played, the records of both the home and away teams, and the location of the game.

The News Page will allow you to see all recent and past news regarding the Bordentown Baseball team. From reports on wins, to updates in the rankings and schedule of the team, to write-ups about players and performances, the News Page will give you the tools you need to stay up-to-date with the Bordentown Baseball team.
